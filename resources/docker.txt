3. SSL  - self signed certificate
	openssl req -x509 -nodes -days 365 -newkey rsa:2048 -keyout certs/voinard.key -out certs/voinard.crt -subj "/C=RO/ST=Bucuresti/L=Bucuresti/O=Voinard/OU=Dev/CN=voinard.local"
	openssl req -x509 -nodes -days 365 -newkey rsa:2048 -keyout certs/site_altul.key -out certs/site_altul.crt -subj "/C=RO/ST=Bucuresti/L=Bucuresti/O=SiteAltul/OU=Dev/CN=site_altul.local"

	WSL vede discurile Windows în /mnt/.
	
	Start- ubuntu

	C: → /mnt/c/
	D: → /mnt/d/
